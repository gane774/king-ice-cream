Our Company

Where Dairy Meets
Royal Craftsmanship

Vijaykant Dairy & Food Products Ltd began in 2006 alongside the river Malaprabha in Belagavi with a simple mission: bring honest, pure dairy to every home. What started at 5,000 litres a day has grown into a 1-lakh-litre daily operation — and in 2024, we launched King Ice Cream, our premium brand built on two decades of dairy mastery.

2004

Foundation

Vijaykant Dairy & Food Products Ltd incorporated in Belagavi, Karnataka.

2006

Operations Begin

Processing starts at 5,000 L/day. European-grade machinery installed at Neginhal.

2014

National Recognition

Gold & Silver Medals at The Great Indian Ice Cream Contest by DANISCO.

2024

King Ice Cream Launches

Own brand launched across Karnataka, Maharashtra, Goa & Kerala.

2026

₹45 Cr Expansion

New 1-lakh-LPD processing facility under construction with MoFPI subsidy.

Leadership

Two Decades of
Vision & Purpose

Mrs. Deepa Sidnal leads Vijaykant Dairy & Food Products Ltd with over 20 years of hands-on experience in the dairy industry. Under her stewardship, the company scaled from a local processor to a multi-state dairy powerhouse.

Her son, Mr. Vijaykant Sidnal, manages marketing and operations, bringing a new generation's drive to a trusted legacy. The board is further strengthened by Mr. Vijay Sankeshwar, Chairman of VRL Logistics Limited.

"Our dairy has always been about honesty, quality, and building trust — one litre, one scoop at a time. King Ice Cream is the natural extension of everything we stand for."

— Mrs. Deepa Sidnal, Managing Director

Our Edge

What Sets
King Apart

In a market flooded with frozen desserts cut with vegetable oils and artificial additives, King Ice Cream stands on authentic dairy craftsmanship, international infrastructure, and a legacy that predates most competitors by decades.

🥇 Gold Medal🥈 Silver Medal

The Great Indian Ice Cream Contest

Awarded by DANISCO — a world-renowned Danish ingredient company — recognising King's exceptional quality, texture, and taste among hundreds of national brands.

01

100% Real Dairy Milk — No Vegetable Oil

While many brands substitute vegetable oil for milk fat, King uses genuine full-fat dairy milk from Karnataka farmers — richer, creamier, and nutritionally superior.

Purity Guarantee
02

European-Grade Machinery — Sweden & Italy

Our Neginhal plant runs on imported equipment from Sweden and Italy. Precision pasteurisation, homogenisation, and freezing at every step.

World-Class Infrastructure
03

Rigorous In-House Quality Lab

Every batch is tested in our QC laboratory before leaving the plant — from fat content to microbial load. Nothing ships until it meets our exacting standards.

Zero-Compromise Quality
04

Farmer-First Supply Chain

We directly source fresh milk from thousands of farmers in Belagavi district — fair prices, rural employment, and the shortest possible farm-to-factory chain.
